Confounding Tools

Brief:Design a poster about life under COVID 19 without using software explicitly intended for design.

The lost of balance caused by COVID-19, it would be hard to go back to the days before the pandemic.

Concept 1

Covid-19 experienced several waves and lockdown again and again, which made me feel tired. It's like a game that can never be finished.

software: pixilart

Concept 2

Use some common objects in life to express the unbalanced state. For example, a floating chair or a fallen flower can be a metaphor for the imbalance between work and life.

software: Studio2.0

FIRST POSTER DRAFT

COVID-19 lasted for a long time, and it has changed the way of our lives, work, study, etc. Similar reports began to appear in the news: the pandemic called for and stimulated the arrival of the meta-universe.
